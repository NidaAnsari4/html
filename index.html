Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for delicious rich scrambled eggs.

Ingridents

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from edge of the center every 20 sceond (as if you are making)
  5. Stirring frequently until the eggs are set but still creamy.
